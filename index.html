The Many Sidekicks Of Batman

Over the years Batman & Robin have been prominent in DC comics.

However there are many people who have gone by Robin in addition to various sidekicks and allies with different titles.

Robin is the most widely known character to be paired with Batman, but there are five diferent people to have the title.

    Robins:

  • Richard Grayson
  • Jason Todd
  • Tim Drake
  • Stephanie Brown
  • Damian Wayne
